Welcome to the Digital Industrial Revolution

The Digital Industrial Revolution


Gone are the days when the Factory powered the Middle Class. The Digital Industrial Revolution has arrived. And while the Revolution has made science fiction a realitly, it also created a new skill to nessacary to thrive.


Digital Literacy

The ability to consume, create, and collaborate in a digital environment, Digital Literacy has become the cornerstone of economic opportunity in the 21st century. Sadly, with developers numbering less than .02% percent of the global population, digital literacy seems out of reach for all but the most fourtnate individuals. It doesn't have to be this way.


Indigenous Code


Indigenous Code is a group of individuals dedicated to ensuring the digital world isn't closed off to a select few. A network of Agile Education and Development Teams, we are both a Community and a Movement. Our mission is to use coding to empower those who have been disempowered. Ensuring that togther we can all reap the rewards of the Digital Industrial Revolution.


The Indigenous Code Framework


At Indigenous Code we have created a framework by which individual, regardless of background or experience can gain digital literacy, and become a citizen of digital world.


Using online tools and agile methodologies, Indigenous Code creates an individual education plan for anyone free of change. Then individuals are incorporated into eduSCRUM teams, where they work together to increase their capabilities in the digital world.

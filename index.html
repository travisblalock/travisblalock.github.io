The Digital Industrial Revolution


Indigenous Code is an organization dedicated to empowering individuals to thrive in the digital workd. Gone are the days when the Factory powered the Middle Class. In the digital world, people need a new way to succeed. Through a network of Agile Software development and education teams Indigenous Code strives create that new way for people to succeed.


Forget About $15, Make $30!


Indigenous Code is tearing down the barriers to economic opportunity. Members of one of Indigenous Code’s software development teams receive a starting salary equivalent to 30 dollars an hour.
